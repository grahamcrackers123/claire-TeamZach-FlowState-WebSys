Overview

Productivity is a very concept to fathom in a world with a plethora of distractions. According to behavioral theory, a boring action can be executed more enjoyably if you track your progress. Our solution? Welcome to the FlowState Productivity Tracker! We have an assortment of uses that you can navigate to below. We can analyze your productivity in the Calculate Productivity Page, or we can see your progress throughout the days in the History Page. If you want to know more about us, feel free to go to the About Us page.
